HOW TO SURVIVE

01📦
ACQUIRE

Buy archives on Cairo's black market, run faction quests, intercept live data leaks, or send agents on covert operations. Every source carries different risk.

02🔍
ANALYZE

Open files — text, audio, video, images. Decode encrypted data, merge fragments, identify memory shards. Viruses lurk in the shadows of every archive.

03⚡
TRADE

Sell to Corporations, Rebels, or Hackers across Cairo's 19 districts. Trust multiplies profit. Betrayal costs reputation. Every deal reshapes the power balance.

04🤖
DEPLOY

Build a team of agents — Chrome, Intellect, Strength, Agility. Send them on raids and infiltrations. Manage cyberpsychosis. Always expect betrayal.

WHO IS
BRUCE?

The year is 2127. Cairo — once a city of ancient pharaohs — is now a megacity of neon minarets, drone-polluted skies, and data wars waged in the shadow of the pyramids.

Bruce wakes up in the gutters of District 4 with no memory, no face, no past. A weathered data trader named Tork pulls him off the streets and into the underground economy of stolen information.

Every archive he cracks holds a fragment of who he was. Corporations want him buried. Rebels want his secrets. Hackers just want to watch it all burn.
